**YouMatter** is an AI-driven mental health support web application designed specifically for students. It provides a safe, private, and judgment-free space where students can express their feelings, seek emotional support, and access relevant mental health resources anytime, anywhere.

## ğŸš¨ Problem Statement

Students today face intense academic pressure, social expectations, financial stress, and uncertainty about their future. These challenges have significantly increased cases of anxiety, depression, burnout, and emotional distress.

Despite the growing need, access to mental health support remains limited due to:

* Social stigma around mental health
* Shortage of counselors on campuses
* Long waiting times for appointments
